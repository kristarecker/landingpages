🧠 Why this approach

Cash offers are built from a formula. Your sale should be too.

Most cash investors price a property by subtracting their rehab budget, their carrying costs, and their profit margin from what they think it could sell for. The math is real. The problem is, it belongs to them, not to you. The Decision Sheet runs the same math from your side of the table, so the offer is no longer the only number in the conversation. Sometimes the cash offer is the right call. Sometimes the as-is on market path nets meaningfully more. The framework tells you which.
